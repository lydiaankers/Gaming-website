Planet Coaster is a construction and management simulation video game in which There are four different modes in the game, namely Sandbox, Challenge, Career, and Scenario Editor. In the career mode, players assume the role of a theme park manager and must complete tasks such as constructing unfinished roller coasters or hiring janitors. In Sandbox mode, players are tasked to construct their own theme park on an empty plot of land. The challenge mode is similar to the sandbox mode, but with added difficulty, as players need to take funds into account. The Scenario Mode, which was added in the 1.4.0 update on November 22, 2017, is similar to career mode, but allow player customise their own task in all saved parks.
