Playing through this game through the perspective of a struggling couple and allowing you to see them work out through their problems as you progress in the game creates strong feeling and unforgetable moments in the game. The visuals for the game are bright, unique and interesting as it feels like you are traveling through a childs world with many contraptions to capture your attention. this game combined all different generes of games as jokes allowing for a fresh feeling to the whole game as well as the crazy style making everything interesting as a player. This game has a special style of platforming as you will need to work with your partner every step of the way to progress to the next section making for some frustrating times aswell as joyus times. Overall, this game evokes many emotions out of its players as they make many situations relatable especially through the chraracters eyes.
